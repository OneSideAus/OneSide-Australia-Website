Why OneSide exists

A gap that needed filling.

Sporting clubs across Australia were required to meet a broad, complex set of child safe requirements, but the practical support to help them do it simply didn't exist. Generic frameworks, policy templates, and fact sheets were everywhere. A clear, achievable path built around how clubs actually operate was nowhere to be found.

Clubs were not failing for lack of care. Committees of volunteers give their weekends to make sport happen for children. They want to get child safety right. What they lacked was a practical, club-specific path to get there.

OneSide was built by someone who had seen that gap firsthand, from inside sporting clubs, not from a distance, and who had the expertise to fill it.

The name

"Because when it comes to protecting children, there is only OneSide."

When it comes to the safety of children, there are no two sides to the debate. No competing interests, no grey areas, no reasons to delay. There is only one side, and it's the side of the child. That belief drives everything about how OneSide works.

Statement of intent

"OneSide Australia is committed to working alongside sporting clubs nationwide to put the right structures, policies, and processes in place, because when the foundations are built correctly, a culture of child safety follows naturally."

We don't just identify gaps. We help clubs build the framework that makes genuine child safety possible, sustainable, and embedded in everything they do.

Our values

What we stand for.

Six values that guide every assessment, every training session, and every decision we make.

Children first
"Children are not a consideration, they are the reason."
Every piece of advice we give, every training session we run, and every report we write starts and ends with one question: is this making things safer for children?
Integrity
Real child safety goes beyond policies on paper.
We hold ourselves to the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and accountability, because children deserve more than a tick-box exercise.
Expertise you can trust
Built on firsthand experience where it matters most.
OneSide was built on firsthand experience working at the intersection of child safety, community, and sport. We understand how systems work, where they fall short, and what clubs actually need to get it right.
Courage
Self-reflection takes courage. So does committing to change.
We match that courage by delivering clear, complete assessments and empowering clubs with the tools, knowledge, and confidence to shape a culture where child safety is a priority, not an afterthought.
Inclusion
Every child deserves to feel safe, valued, and protected.
Regardless of ability, culture, or background. Our work is inclusive by design. We support clubs of all sizes, all sports, and all communities across Australia to meet their child safety obligations.
Empowerment
We don't just identify problems, we build capability.
We work alongside clubs to put the right structures in place so that child safety becomes self-sustaining, and every club is stronger, more confident, and better equipped long after our work together is done.
