How the assessment works

Everything your club has, measured against everything it needs.

The assessment goes deeper than a checklist. You share your existing information — policies written and unwritten, practices formal and informal — and OneSide measures the full picture against national and sport-specific requirements. What comes back is a prioritised gap report built around how your club actually operates.

What goes in

  • Your existing written policies and procedures
  • Unwritten practices and how your club operates
  • Structured questions across all key standard areas
  • Your state, sport, and club size

What comes out

  • A gap report specific to your club
  • Gaps identified by standard area
  • Prioritised, achievable actions
  • Sport and state-specific guidance
  • A foundation for annual reassessment

Always current

  • Updated when standards change
  • Reflects the latest regulatory requirements
  • Built for annual reassessment
  • Your report is a living benchmark
Your report belongs to you
OneSide does not share your gap report with your sporting body, regulator, or any third party. Ever.
Safe to be honest
OneSide has no reporting obligation based on your responses. The more accurate your answers, the more useful your report.
A self-assessment, not a verdict
Your club is doing the self-assessment. OneSide's framework is the measuring stick, not the judge.
